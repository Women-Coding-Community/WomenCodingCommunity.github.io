Why Volunteer With Us ?

  • Support Women in Tech Help create an inclusive environment where women can thrive in technology.
  • Share Your Expertise Use your knowledge and skills to mentor others, lead workshops, or support our events.
  • Build Connections Join a network of like-minded individuals who are passionate about making a positive impact.
  • Develop New Skills Gain valuable experience in teaching, mentoring, event planning, and more.

Volunteer Opportunities At A Glance

  • Organisational Skills Assist in organizing meetups, online tech events, and other community activities.
  • Show Off Your Tech Skills Collaborate with us to build our new platform or enhance features on our existing website.
  • Enhance Your Writing Skills Contribute to our content creation efforts, while connecting with others who share your passion for positive change.
  • Develop Your Speaking Skills Share your insights with the community through talks or presentations. We’ll host an event for you, and offer support to help you get started if needed.
