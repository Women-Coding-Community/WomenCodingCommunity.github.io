Writing Club

Are you passionate about blogging, eager to share your ideas, and looking to improve your writing skills?

The Online Writing Club is your perfect destination to practise blog posting, collaborate with fellow writers, and refine your craft—all from the comfort of your home.

Whether you’re a seasoned blogger, an aspiring writer, or simply someone who loves to share stories and ideas, our club offers a supportive and stimulating environment for everyone.

What we offer

{% include icons/plus.svg %}
  • Monthly Blog Writing Sessions: Join our live, interactive monthly sessions where members come together to write blog posts in real-time. Benefit from a focused and collaborative atmosphere to boost your productivity and creativity.
  • Peer Reviews and Feedback: Share your blog posts with fellow members and receive constructive feedback. Improve your writing style, readability, and engagement with insights from a supportive community.
  • Writing Workshops and Tutorials: Attend online workshops focused on blog writing, covering topics like content creation, infographics, and audience engagement. Learn from experienced bloggers to enhance your writing skills.
  • Guest Speaker Sessions: Gain inspiration and tips from guest speakers who are successful bloggers, content creators, and influencers. Learn about the latest trends, best practices, and strategies in the blogging world.
  • Collaborative Projects: Work on group blogging projects, guest posts, and co-authored series with other members. Expand your network and reach by collaborating on exciting and diverse projects.
  • Networking and Community: Connect with like-minded individuals in our virtual networking events and slack channel. Build a network of friends, mentors, and collaborators who share your passion for writing.

Join us in our Writing Club Slack channel

Speaking Club

Are you looking to improve your public speaking skills, enhance your presentation abilities, and gain confidence in delivering talks?

The Online Speaking Club is your ultimate platform to practise and refine your speaking skills in a supportive and interactive virtual environment.

Whether you’re preparing for a professional presentation, a public talk, or simply want to improve your communication skills, our club offers the resources and community you need to succeed.

What we offer

{% include icons/plus.svg %}
  • Practice Sessions: Join regular practice sessions where you can deliver your talks and presentations to a supportive audience. Get immediate feedback and constructive criticism to help you improve.
  • Speech Evaluations and Feedback: Receive detailed evaluations from peers and mentors to refine your delivery and content. Use personalised feedback to enhance your strengths and address areas for improvement.
  • Guest Speaker Events: Gain insights from guest speakers who are experts in public speaking, presentation skills, and communication. Learn best practices and tips from successful orators and industry leaders.
  • Online Debates and Discussions: Engage in structured online debates to sharpen your argumentation and persuasive speaking skills. Participate in group discussions on a variety of topics to improve your ability to articulate ideas clearly.
  • Webinars and Q&A Sessions: Attend webinars covering various aspects of public speaking and presentation techniques. Participate in Q&A sessions with experienced speakers to get your questions answered.
  • Networking and Community:Connect with like-minded individuals in our virtual networking events and slack channel Build a network of friends, mentors, and collaborators who share your passion for speaking.

Join us in our Speaking Club Slack channel

The Coding Club is your ideal community to dive deep into the world of software development, explore cutting-edge technologies, and connect with fellow coding enthusiasts.

Whether you’re a seasoned developer, a curious beginner, or somewhere in between, our club offers a stimulating and supportive environment for everyone.

What we offer

{% include icons/plus.svg %}
  • Workshops and Tutorials: Participate in hands-on workshops led by experienced developers and industry professionals. Attend tutorials on various programming languages, frameworks, and tools, from Python and JavaScript to AI and machine learning.

  • Networking and Online Events:Connect with like-minded individuals at our coding sessions and on the slack channel!
