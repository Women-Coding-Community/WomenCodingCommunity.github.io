Welcome to the Machine Learning Bootcamp!

Are you eager to dive into the world of Machine Learning, solve real-world problems, and enhance your data science skills? Our Machine Learning Bootcamp is the perfect platform for both beginners and experienced practitioners to collaborate, learn, and grow in the field of machine learning

Over the course of six weeks, you'll work on a series of engaging and challenging problems, guided by experienced mentors and supported by a vibrant community of learners.

What we offer

{% include icons/plus.svg %}
  • Weekly Problem-Solving Sessions:
    • Gather with fellow participants for 2-hour interactive sessions each week.
    • Tackle one unique machine learning problem, applying the concepts you've learned in real-time.
    • Work collaboratively in a supportive environment where you can ask questions, share ideas, and learn from others.
  • Guided Learning and Mentorship:
    • Receive guidance from experienced mentors who will help you navigate the complexities of machine learning.
    • Get personalised feedback on your solutions, with tips on how to improve and optimise your models.
  • Hands-On Projects:
    • Apply your learning to hands-on projects that simulate real-world machine learning tasks.
    • Build a portfolio of projects that demonstrate your skills and knowledge in machine learning.
  • Guest Speaker Sessions:
    • Gain insights from industry experts and thought leaders in the field of machine learning.
    • Learn about the latest trends, tools, and techniques that are shaping the future of AI and machine learning.
  • Networking and Community:
    • Connect with like-minded individuals in our virtual networking events and dedicated Slack channel.
    • Build relationships with peers, mentors, and professionals who share your passion for machine learning.

Join us in this exciting journey to master machine learning, solve complex problems and take your skills to the next level. Whether you're looking to kickstart a career in AI or enhance your existing knowledge, our bootcamp offers the resources, support, and community you need to succeed.
